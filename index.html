Software Enginering is the design, development, release, measurement, maintenance, analysis and evolution of secure, user & task-focused software systems and processes; and in Portsmouth you can study to become a Bachelor of Science (with Honours) in Software Engineering.

In year one we focus on capability: becoming a programmer, development of core skills and discovering reliable knowledge

In year two students learn to apply their skills, becoming an engineer and using reliable knowledge

In year three we are able to concentrate more on becoming a scientist; researching, critiquing and contributing to reliable knowledge
